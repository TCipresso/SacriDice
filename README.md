"SacriDice" is a video game made by Fistbump Studios for the Brackeys Game Jam 2025.2.

Fistbump Studios Developers:
Tommaso Cipresso - Software Engineer.
Anthony Lira - Software engineer / musician. 
Colby Loner - Artist.

Playable Game Link: https://fistbump-studios.itch.io/sacridice

A round-based strategy game where your survival hinges on the roll of your dice. Between rounds, gamble your earnings to add new dice to your arsenal, bolstering your odds for the ante ahead. 
Roll your fate, spend your fortune, you might die.
